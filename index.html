This portal is built for authorized members of the group to create cleaner, more consistent promotion and demotion logs. The system keeps the same core fields as the original log layout while making them easier to fill out correctly and easier to review afterward.

Log a promotion or demotion

Complete every field carefully. The goal is to make entries clear enough that another authorized person could review the log later and immediately understand who was changed, what rank was involved, why the action happened, whether a cooldown applies, and who officially recorded it.
